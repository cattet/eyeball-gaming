how to use this sim

p6 wroth flames priorities

this app makes use of the same priority system as the NAUR toolbox and Saemi's raidplan:

m2   m1   r2   r1   h2   h1   t2   t1

party list customizations

users can do the following to make this app more useful for practice:

  • choose the active party member (you!)
    • click the star button next to the party member most appropriate for your role in your static.
  • reorder the party list
    • simply drag and drop. the order will be saved in your browser.
  • set the names of each party member
    • click settingscustomize party on the bottom right to open up a dialog and enter names that suit your fancy. these names will persist in your browser until you clear your site data.
  • change the class of each party member future
  • change group assignments of each party member future

using the sim

on load, the following statuses will be randomly assigned to appropriate party members and start counting down.

  • wroth flames debuffs
    • spread
    • stack
    • nothing
  • vow pass debuffs
    • vow
    • passed vow
  • healing and mitigation
  • class-specific personal buffs

the reasoning behind this is that half the challenge of wroth flames is in finding the debuffs on yourself and the party in the first place.

after you think you know what your spot will be in the hot wing / hot tail lineup, click on the corresponding aoe circle in the arena panel to see if you are right.

click shufflewe go agane to reroll the statuses and try again.

arena options

the arena is to scale. the boss hitboxes, waymarks, and aoes are rendered to match in-game dimensions so you can orient yourself to any of these landmarks.

to show or hide boss hitboxes, click on the axolotl boss you would like to target. only one boss can be selected at a time to simulate what is actually visible in game. you can click anywhere else in the arena to de-target them.

to show or hide waymarks, toggle the slider on the bottom right of the arena. these are off by default to reduce visual clutter.
